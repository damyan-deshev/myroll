# Myroll Static Asset License Summary

This file is a human-readable summary for Myroll's bundled and static creative assets. The full legal terms are in [LICENSE.md](LICENSE.md). If this summary and the full license conflict, [LICENSE.md](LICENSE.md) controls.

## Plain-English Intent

Use the assets at the table. Use them in your streamed or recorded games. Modify them. Share free forks and free community packs. Do paid creative, technical, hosting, or GM work with them as tools.

Do not resell Myroll's assets as assets.

Covered assets include bundled battle maps, future character and NPC portraits, token art, screenshots, demo assets, manifests, metadata, and related static content committed to this repository, unless a file or directory clearly states a different license.

## You May

- use the assets in private games, public games, paid GM sessions, convention games, classroom/library games, and similar tabletop RPG play;
- use the assets in actual-play streams, recorded sessions, videos, podcasts, blog posts, tutorials, reviews, Patreon/Ko-fi/community posts, and other play or commentary contexts;
- copy, fork, mirror, archive, crop, resize, recolor, edit, remix, and adapt the assets;
- redistribute original or modified assets for free;
- include the assets in free campaign kits, free community bundles, free forks, and free archival copies;
- charge for your own GM time, campaign preparation, installation help, support, customization work, hosting for a specific table, or other labor, as long as access to the assets themselves is not the product being sold.

## You May Not

Without separate written permission from Damyan Deshev, you may not:

- sell, license, rent, lease, or charge for access to the assets as a map pack, token pack, portrait pack, stock-art pack, marketplace item, VTT marketplace module, downloadable paid bundle, paid database, or substantially similar product;
- place the assets behind a paywall where access to those assets is the thing being sold;
- package the assets into a commercial product whose primary value comes from the Myroll assets;
- imply that Damyan Deshev officially endorses your commercial service, product, marketplace listing, or organization.
